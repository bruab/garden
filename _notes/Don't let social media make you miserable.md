Advice from a random NCBI paper¹:

Seek to maximize:
- Positive interactions
- Social support
- Social connectedness

Seek to minimize:
- Negative interactions
- Social comparisons

¹ [source](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5143470/)
